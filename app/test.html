There are four ways to register for GRE Test :

1. Phone

For applicants in U.S, U.S territory and Canada:

You can call at 1-443-751-4820 or 1-800-GRE-CALL (1-800-473-2255) on weekdays between 8 a.m to 8 p.m.

For payment you can use either American Express, Discover, JCB, MasterCard, VISA or a voucher number.

For applicants located at other locations:

Call the appropriate RRC (Regional Registration center). Click here to get list of RRC.

Mode of Payment: A valid credit card either American Express, JBC, Discover, MasterCard or VISA. Make sure to call at least two business days before the test date. You will receive the confirmation number and test center address after registration.

2. Online Registration:

For registering online you need to have or create a My GRE Account to register online.
Click on the following link to register online: http://ets.org/gre/grereg

Mode of Payment (Credit Card only)
You will require American Express, Discover, JCB, MasterCard, VISA credit card.

The Online Registration facility is not available in Nigeria.

After registering online, you can check your appointment confirmation at https://securereg3.prometric.com/RescheduleOrCancel.aspx?mode=

3. Mail

For applicants in U.S, U.S territory and Canada:

Download the Computer Based Authorization Voucher Request Form.

Mail it to the following address along with the the payment:

ETS-CBT/GRE
Box 371859
Pittsburgh, PA 15250-7859

It may take around four weeks for processing. After you receive your authorization voucher, you can call to schedule your appointment. The appointment must be made before the expiration date given on voucher.

The preferred mode of payment is money order, certified check or personal check.

For applicants located at other locations:

Download the International Scheduling Form and mail it to the appropriate RRC. Make sure that the documents are received at least three weeks before the test date.

Click here to get list of RRC.

You can pay either through a credit card, money order or a certified check.

4. Fax

Fax registration is not available for test takers in Mainland China, Hong Kong, Taiwan and Korea

Download the International Scheduling Form and fax it to the appropriate RRC.
Click here to get list of RRC

The fax should be received at least seven days before the first test date.

Mode of Payment: American Express, Discover, JCB, MasterCard, VISA, or a voucher number.

When registering through Fax or mail, a confirmation number and test center address will be send to you. In case if it is not received call the appropriate RRC at least three business days before the test date.

To get more details on registering, click Register for GRE on http://www.gre.org. Most of the information here was taken from there. Click here to get GRE Bulletin.

Rescheduling or Canceling Test Appointment:

For applicants located outside U.S:
Contact the appropriate RRC at least three business days before the test date.

For applicants in U.S, U.S Territory or Canada:
Call 1-443-751-4820 or 1-800-GRE-CALL (1-800-473-2255) or the local center where you are scheduled to test at least three business days before the test date.

Important Points:

  1. Register early so that your scores are received before you want to apply in colleges.
  2. Make sure that while registering, the spellings of name matches with the one printed on the ID that you will present at the test center.
  3. Late registration is available if you register online. Late registration closes one week after the regular registration date.
  4. You can get list of test centers all over the world at https://etsis4.ets.org/tcenter/
